## Step 6 — Apply the Efficiency Staircase (Round by Round)

Fill your bracket from the early rounds forward. At each stage, check whether the team you're picking meets the efficiency floor for that round.

### Round of 64 Picks
- Pick KenPom inversions as upsets (Step 3)
- Pick against red-flagged favorites (Step 4)
- Default to the higher seed when no signal exists
- **11 vs 6 matchups:** Lean 11-seed (63.5% ATS since 2009) — check if the 11 is a power conference mis-seed
- **First Four winners:** Back them in R64 (92.1% ATS since 2013)

### Round of 32 Picks
- **Efficiency floor: AdjEM +14 to +16**
- Fade R64 Cinderellas (12–15 seeds are 0-10 ATS in R32 since 1998)
- Back 10/11-seeds that won R64 (13-5 ATS since 2016)
- Check AdjD — must be top 55 to survive

### Sweet 16 Picks
- **Efficiency floor: AdjEM +22 to +24, AdjD top 40**
- This is the steepest cliff — the +14 to +22 AdjEM gap eliminates most mid-tier teams
- Fade 5+ point favorites (31.8% ATS)
- Back 4–6 point underdogs (56.7% ATS since 1990)
- Seeds 6–9: only 7.5% of Sweet 16 spots — fade them unless they pass Cinderella profile

### Elite 8 Picks
- **Efficiency floor: AdjEM +20, both AdjO and AdjD top 30**
- One-dimensional teams get exposed here — both ends of the floor required
- Lean underdogs overall (57% ATS all-time)
- **5-seeds in Elite 8: 8-1 ATS since 1985** — best historical edge
- Fade 1-seeds (43.4% ATS) — market overprices them

### Final Four Picks
- **Efficiency floor: AdjEM +25, AdjD top 25**
- Only your champion gate teams should be here
- Must be matchup-proof on both ends

### Championship Pick
- Pick the Final Four winner to cover — 83% ATS (35-7-2 since 2001)
- Your champion should come from Step 1's contender list

## Step 7 — Final Bracket Review Checklist

Before you submit, run through these sanity checks:

- [ ] Champion passes all 8 gates from Step 1
- [ ] No "Extreme Team" (top 10 one side, 50+ other) in your Final Four
- [ ] At least one 11-seed in your Sweet 16 (they've appeared every year)
- [ ] No 13 or 14 seed in your Sweet 16 (0 appearances in 6 years)
- [ ] No more than one 12+ seed past R32 (Cinderellas fade)
- [ ] Every Sweet 16 team has AdjD inside top 40
- [ ] Every Elite 8 team has both AdjO and AdjD inside top 30
- [ ] At least one "upset" in each region's R64 (average is 5–7 total upsets)
- [ ] Checked for injuries announced after Selection Sunday
- [ ] Your champion is from a power conference (ACC, Big 12, Big East, SEC)
