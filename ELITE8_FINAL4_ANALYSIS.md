## Part 6 ‚Äî What Separates Final Four from Sweet 16 (The Delta)

| Delta | Sweet 16 Teams | Final Four Teams |
|-------|---------------|-----------------|
| KenPom Rank | Top 5-40 | Top 1-15 |
| AdjEM | +18 to +28 | +25 to +36 |
| AdjO Rank | 15-60 | 5-30 |
| AdjD Rank | 10-55 | 5-30 |
| Both O+D top 30 | Uncommon | Near-universal |
| Effective FG% | 20-80 | 5-40 |
| Free throw rate | Variable | Top 30 |

**The 7 Critical Separators:**

1. **Two-way efficiency gap** ‚Äî Final Four teams are top 20 in BOTH AdjO and AdjD. Sweet 16 teams often rank 40-80 on one side.
2. **AdjEM above +25** ‚Äî The structural breakpoint. Below +20 is a long shot for Final Four regardless of seed.
3. **Consistent winning margin** ‚Äî Champions win by a lot (UConn 2024: 23.3 avg margin). Teams surviving close games eventually lose one.
4. **Free throw generation** ‚Äî All 6 champions were top-25 in free throw attempt rate. Getting to the line absorbs variance in close games.
5. **Interior presence or defensive scheme** ‚Äî Every champion had a dominant big, elite paint control, or comprehensive defensive system.
6. **Schedule-tested r√©sum√©** ‚Äî 23 of last 24 champions had SOS inside the top 33. Weak schedules do not prepare teams for back-to-back elite opponents.
7. **Proven head coach** ‚Äî Every 2019-2025 champion was coached by a multiple-tournament-appearance veteran (Bennett, Drew, Self, Hurley x2, Golden).

## Part 8 ‚Äî Recommendations for Betting Model

### Champion Predictor Gate (Must meet ALL):
- [ ] KenPom top 6 at tournament time
- [ ] AdjEM above +25
- [ ] AdjO top 25
- [ ] AdjD top 25
- [ ] SOS top 33
- [ ] Seed 1-4
- [ ] Power conference (ACC/Big 12/Big East/SEC)

### ATS Model Triggers:

| Situation | Action | Confidence |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| 5-seed in Elite 8 | Auto-buy underdog ATS | Very High |
| 1-seed favored in Elite 8 | Fade on spread | High |
| Elite 8 underdog 3+ pts | Take the points | High |
| Non-power conference in Elite 8 | Take them ATS | High |
| Final Four ‚Äî pick the winner | They'll cover 83% | High |
| Final Four underdog 4-6 pts | Take the points | Moderate |
| Both teams seed 3+ in Elite 8 | Take higher-numbered seed | Moderate |

### Stat Inputs for Model (Ranked by Impact):
1. KenPom Overall Rank (hard gate: top 6 for champion, top 15 for Final Four)
2. AdjEM value (threshold: +25 for Final Four, +29 for champion)
3. AdjO rank (top 25 required for champion)
4. AdjD rank (top 25 required for champion)
5. Free throw attempt rate (top 25 = signal)
6. SOS rank (top 33 = gate for champion-level picks)
7. Effective field goal % (top 20 = champion signal)
8. Tournament seed (1-seed flags, 5-seed ATS trigger, 11-seed Cinderella flag)
9. Conference tier (ACC/Big 12/Big East/SEC = Tier 1)
10. Head coach tournament experience (experienced coach = +)
